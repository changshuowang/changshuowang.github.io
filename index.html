Changshuo Wang (王昌硕)

In 2023, I obtained my doctoral degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and the Institute of Semiconductors, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), advised by Prof. Weijun Li . In 2018, I obtained my B.Eng. in the institute of automation, Qingdao University of science and technology (QUST). In September 2023, I will join in Nanyang Technological University (NTU) to become a Research Fellow.

I am interested in computer vision and deep learning. My current research focuses on:

  • Design and development of 2D/3D scene reconstruction and understanding algorithms based on images/lidar point clouds, mainly applied in the areas of autonomous driving, robotics, remote sensing, and AR/VR;
  • Research on deep learning-based brain-inspired visual cognition algorithm design.
